02 — Icon Anatomy
Logo Concept Breakdown
Every element of the icon carries meaning tied to property management automation.

Blue Tower — The Landlord

Represents the property owner. Electric blue signals trust, technology, and reliability. Slightly shorter — grounded, stable.

Gold Tower — The Property

Represents the asset being managed. Gold signals value, income, and premium service. Taller — showing growth potential.

Window Grid — Data & Units

Each window represents a rental unit, tenant record, or data point. The grid pattern evokes a dashboard — organized, monitored, automated.

Twin Structure — HQ

Two buildings side by side form an "H" negative space, reinforcing the "HQ" — your command center for all properties.

05 — Brand Voice
How LandlordHQ Speaks
The voice should feel like a sharp, trusted business partner — not a robot, not a salesman.
🎯

Direct & Clear

No jargon, no fluff. Filipino landlords are busy — respect their time. Say "Track rent in 2 taps" not "Leverage our comprehensive receivables module."

🤝

Confident, Not Cocky

We know property management. We've automated the tedious parts. But we never talk down to landlords — they know their business better than anyone.

⚡

Action-Oriented

Every message should push toward a result. "Set up in 5 minutes." "See your first report today." Verbs over adjectives, always.
